Can't Sleep? Try This Gentle Night Reset

🛏️ Sleep Hygiene

Go to bed and wake up at the same time every day. Keep your room cool, dark, and quiet. Avoid caffeine late in the day.

📵 Wind Down

Replace screen time with a 10-minute audio or breathing exercise. Try reading or stretching before bed.

🖊️ Journaling

Write for 5 minutes before bed. Let go of thoughts and tasks so your mind can rest.
